Welcome to my homepage!

My name is Kai Xu (徐凯). I am an Assistant Professor at School of Computer Science, National University of Defense Technology (NUDT). I earned my Ph.D in computer science in June 2011 from NUDT. From Nov. 2008 to Oct. 2010, I enjoyed a two-year happy time at the GrUVi lab of Simon Fraser University (SFU), Canada, working with Prof. Richard (Hao) Zhang. Here is my CV (updated on 2015-08-12).

My research interests are in the areas of computer graphics, especially in geometry processing.
My current topics of interests include shape analysis, high-level geometry processing, and data-driven shape modeling. You are welcomed to take a look at my publication page.
